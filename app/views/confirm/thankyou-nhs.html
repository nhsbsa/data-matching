Your NHS Number

We still need your NHS Number. You can find your NHS Number on most documents sent to you by the NHS, such as your:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral or appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • on your NHS medical card​

Or, contact your GP surgery and ask them what your NHS Number is.

Could I receive a penalty charge?

We match exemptions automatically using a combination of your:

  • name
  • address
  • date of birth
  • NHS Number

Make sure these are correct at your GP surgery, dental practice, pharmacy and any benefits organisations.
