Where to find your NHS Number

If you're registered with a GP, you will have a 10 digit NHS Number unique to you.

It is usually on any letter or document you have received from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes
  • on your NHS medical card​
